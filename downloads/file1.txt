Classic Tomato Spaghetti:
- Ingredients: spaghetti, 2 ripe tomatoes, 1 clove garlic, olive oil, basil, salt, and pepper.
- Instructions: Boil spaghetti in salted water. Chop tomatoes and garlic. Heat oil in a pan, fry garlic until golden, add tomatoes, and cook until tender. Drain spaghetti and mix with the sauce. Garnish with basil leaves.
